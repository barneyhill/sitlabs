About Genorxiv

Genorxiv is a genome browser for visualising genetic findings from bioRxiv & medRxiv. For each SNP mention we asked gpt-4o-mini to summarise the scientific finding given the context of the paper. We then standardise the genome coordinates and map them onto our browser. We wrote a blog describing the process of downloading the preprint data.

Made by the Scientific Interface & Tooling Lab.
